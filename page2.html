How to Manage Stress

Stress is a normal part of life, but it can be managed effectively through various techniques:

  • Deep Breathing: Inhale deeply, hold, and exhale slowly to calm your body.
  • Exercise: Physical activity like walking or yoga releases mood-boosting endorphins.
  • Mindfulness Meditation: Focus on the present moment to reduce stress and clear your mind.
  • Progressive Muscle Relaxation: Tense and relax muscle groups to release physical tension.
  • Time Management: Organize tasks and prioritize to make them more manageable.

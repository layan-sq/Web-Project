Importance of Quality Sleep

Good sleep is essential for mental well-being. It helps improve mood, memory, and overall health...

Tips to Improve Sleep

  • Create a Sleep Routine: Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day.
  • Limit Screen Time: Avoid screens 30 minutes before bed.
  • Keep Bedroom Cool and Dark: Ensure a comfortable room temperature and use blackout curtains.
  • Avoid Caffeine and Heavy Meals: Avoid caffeine 6 hours before bed and large meals before sleeping.
  • Practice Relaxation Techniques: Try deep breathing or reading a calming book.

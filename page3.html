Daily Exercises for Mental Health

Exercise helps reduce anxiety, improve mood, and boost overall mental health. Regular physical activity is essential for both physical and mental well-being. Here are some simple exercises you can do at home:

  • Deep Breathing: Reduces stress and anxiety. Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4, exhale for 6. Repeat for 5-10 minutes.
  • Yoga: Improves focus, balance, and mood. Try simple poses like Child’s Pose, Downward Dog, or Mountain Pose.
  • Progressive Muscle Relaxation: Relieves physical and mental tension. Tense and relax muscle groups starting from your feet up to your head.
  • Walking or Jogging: Boosts mood and energy by releasing endorphins. Walk or jog for 15-30 minutes at a comfortable pace.
  • Aerobic Exercises: Reduces anxiety and depression. Try jumping jacks, skipping rope, or dancing.
  • Guided Meditation: Calms the mind and reduces stress. Follow a 10-20 minute guided meditation video or app.

These exercises can be done at home without any special equipment, and they can help improve your mood, energy levels, and overall health.

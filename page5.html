Effective Relaxation Techniques

Examples of Relaxation Techniques

  • Deep Breathing: Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4, exhale for 6. Repeat for 5 minutes.
  • Meditation: Sit quietly and focus on your breath for 5-10 minutes to calm the mind.
  • Yoga: Try simple poses like Child’s Pose or Downward Dog to release tension.

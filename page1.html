Why Mental Health Matters?

Mental health affects every aspect of our lives, from our relationships to our daily productivity.

  • Relationships: Good mental health improves relationships, while poor mental health causes stress.
  • Daily Productivity: Good mental health increases productivity, while poor mental health reduces focus.
  • Coping with Stress: Good mental health helps handle stress, while poor mental health leads to exhaustion.
  • Decision Making: Good mental health promotes thoughtful decisions, while poor mental health leads to rash choices.
  • Personal Growth: Good mental health encourages growth, while poor mental health hinders it.
